About ATS Systems

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are software applications used by employers to filter and rank resumes before they reach human recruiters.

Keyword Matching

ATS systems scan for specific keywords related to skills and experience.

Format Analysis

Scoring System

ATS Optimization Tips

Do's
  • Use standard section headers
  • Include relevant keywords
  • Save as PDF and plain text
  • Use simple, clean formatting
  • Quantify achievements
Don'ts
  • Avoid graphics and images
  • Don't use tables or columns
  • Avoid unusual fonts
  • Don't use headers/footers
  • Avoid acronyms without explanation
